SEA GRILL SIGNATURE DISHES 

These dishes highlight the creativity and skill of talented chefs in crafting memorable seafood experiences at a Sea Grill restaurant.

Frutti di Mare

Dish for 2 guests

"Frutti di Mare" translates to "fruits of the sea" in Italian, and it is a dish that celebrates the bounty of the ocean. Imagine a plate adorned with strands of black squid ink pasta, which adds a striking visual contrast to the vibrant red sauce that envelops it. The pasta itself is not only visually arresting but also boasts a subtle briny flavor imparted by the squid ink.

Embark on a culinary journey filled with an exquisite mix of premium cheeses, succulent berries, juicy grapes, crunchy nuts, flavorful jam, crackers and sweet honey.

Fried avocado with crab and shrimp

Dive into our mouthwatering Fried Avocado with Blue Crab meat and  Shrimp! Imagine crispy, golden avocado slices paired perfectly with succulent shrimp, diced tomatoes and fresh cucumber. Drizzled with zesty chili mayo for that extra kick

Imperial Ice cream 

 

A decadent ice cream creation made with champagne, cream, condensed milk, lemon zest, and fresh basil. Finished with a lavish topping of black caviar and delicate crawfish. A bold blend of sweet, savory, and indulgent flavors

Royal Catch Tasting

 

6 oz lobster tail, tiger shrimp, scallop, Au gratin potato, served with white wine saffron velouté and topped with black caviar

Chef’s Sashimi Set

Perfect to share for 3-6 guests

Immerse yourself in the Chef’s Sashimi Set—a symphony of the finest cuts!

Indulge in:

Tuna, offering a rich, velvety texture.

Chu-Toro, a succulent medium fatty tuna.

O-Toro, the pinnacle of buttery, melt-in-your-mouth tuna perfection.

Savor:

Salmon, with its delicate flavor and tender flesh.

Hamachi, boasting a buttery texture and subtle sweetness.

Madai ( known for its clean taste and firm texture.

Shimaanji , delivering a delicate balance of flavors.

Delight in:

Ikura, offering bursts of briny goodness.

Japanese Scallops, tender and sweet, with a hint of ocean breeze.

Join us at Sea Grill and embark on a culinary journey through the freshest flavors of the sea meticulously crafted by our chefs! 

Salmon Royale

Indulge in an exquisite pairing of premium salmon belly sashimi and artisan-crafted nigiri, each piece showcasing the buttery richness of the freshest salmon.

This luxurious dish features two slices of melt-in-your-mouth sashimi and two hand-formed nigiri, each delicately crowned with black caviar and shaved fresh truffle.

The briny pop of caviar meets the earthy aroma of truffle, creating a harmonious balance of land and sea.

Every bite is a celebration of flavor, texture, and culinary artistry — a true experience for the refined palate.

SALT-BAKED BRANZINO

Perfect to share for 2-4 guests

Impeccably prepared, a whole large branzino delicately baked in a salt crust to preserve its natural moisture and enhance its flavors. As you break through the crust, discover tender, flaky fish that melts in your mouth.

Accompanied by a medley of sautéed vegetables bursting with color and freshness, and drizzled with a luscious lemon-butter sauce that adds a zesty kick to every bite.

Indulge in this gluten-free masterpiece at Sea Grill restaurant for an unforgettable dining experience that celebrates the art of seafood

SEA GRILL COCKTAILS 

               OCEANIC ELIXIR

Dive into the refreshing depths of our Oceanic Elixir, a tantalizing concoction where floral elegance meets herbal allure. Crafted with a delicate infusion of lavender and rose syrups, this cocktail unfolds with layers of aromatic complexity. The addition of smooth gin and crisp white wine brings a balanced harmony.

              URBAN RIVER 

Embark on a journey through the cityscape with our Urban River cocktail, where the bold spirit of tequila meets the crisp coolness of fresh basil, mint, and cucumber. This invigorating blend offers a refreshing escape, combining the earthy undertones of basil and mint with the rejuvenating essence of cucumber. 

                 BLACK PEARL

Indulge in the opulence of our Black Pearl cocktail, starring the exquisite Dusse cognac. This sophisticated blend unfolds with the deep, smooth notes of cognac, complemented by a hint of mystery and intrigue. Crafted with a touch of elegance, the Black Pearl balances the robust character of Dusse with subtle undertones that linger on the palate.

                WASABI MARTINI

Experience a tantalizing twist on tradition with our Wasabi Martini, where the heat of wasabi meets the herbal freshness of basil. Crafted with premium vodka and a bespoke basil-wasabi mix, this martini delivers a fiery kick tempered by subtle herbal notes. Each sip unfolds with a harmonious balance of flavors, blending the robust intensity of wasabi with fresh basil.

                ROSE MARTINI

Introducing our exquisite Rose Martini, a sophisticated blend of premium vodka infused with delicate rose syrup. This elegant cocktail captivates with its floral aroma and subtly sweet flavor, perfectly balanced by the smoothness of vodka. Each sip is a celebration of romance and refinement, reminiscent of a stroll through a blooming garden.

                   QUEEN 210

Behold the Queen 210, a regal concoction blending the vibrant hues of hibiscus syrup with the elegance of gin and the effervescence of prosecco. Crafted to delight the senses, this cocktail harmonizes the floral notes of hibiscus with the botanicals of gin, culminating in a refreshing and sophisticated flavor profile.

                 SEAFOAM SOUR

Introducing the Seafoam Sour, a captivating cocktail that combines the crisp essence of gin with the exotic flavors of lemongrass blue pea syrup, gently infused with a hint of thyme. This enchanting blend offers a refreshing balance of herbal and citrus notes, evoking the sensation of a coastal breeze.

LYCHEE MARTINI

Indulge in the exotic allure of our Lychee Martini, expertly crafted with premium vodka and the succulent essence of fresh lychee fruit. This sophisticated cocktail balances crisp vodka with the luscious sweetness of lychee, creating a harmonious blend that delights the senses.  It's a perfect choice for sweet lovers.

                      SENSEI

Enter the world of the Sensei cocktail, where the delicate essence of sake meets the soothing qualities of green tea, elegantly infused with the sweetness of honey and the floral touch of rose. This harmonious blend offers a nuanced balance of flavors, combining the smoothness of sake with the earthy notes of green tea, while the honey and rose infusion adds a layer of aromatic sophistication.

ANCHORS AWEIGH OLD FASHION

Set sail with our Anchors Aweigh Old Fashioned, a bold and timeless blend crafted from rich bourbon, luscious black cherry syrup, and aromatic bitters. This classic cocktail embodies the spirit of adventure and nostalgia, offering a perfect balance of smooth bourbon warmth and the sweet tartness of black cherries. Anchors Aweigh Old Fashioned is a tribute to craftsmanship and tradition.

                  WHALE TAIL

Presenting the Whale Tail, a captivating blend of bourbon, ripe bananas, Cointreau, and walnut bitters. This imaginative cocktail combines the rich depth of bourbon with the creamy sweetness of bananas, enhanced by the citrusy notes of Cointreau and the subtle earthiness of walnut bitters. The Whale Tail is a tribute to creativity and craftsmanship

                MESCAL BREEZE

Introducing the Mescal Breeze, a vibrant cocktail blending the smoky allure of mescal with the bright flavors of fresh oranges and pineapples, sweetened with a touch of agave syrup. This refreshing concoction captures the essence of tropical relaxation, offering a harmonious balance of bold earthy tones and citrusy sweetness.
