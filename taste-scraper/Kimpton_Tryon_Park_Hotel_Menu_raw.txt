Experience Luxury at Kimpton Tryon Park Hotel
Explore the Heart of Uptown

Discover Kimpton Tryon Park Hotel, a luxury hotel in Uptown Charlotte, North Carolina, where sophisticated style meets Southern hospitality. Tryon Park offers a boutique experience steps from iconic attractions like Bank of America Stadium, Spectrum Center, and the Charlotte Convention Center, with the vibrant South End just minutes away. Our guestrooms and spacious suites blend modern design with refined comfort, offering a relaxing retreat in the heart of Uptown. Signature Kimpton amenities, thoughtfully curated experiences, and pet-friendly touches ensure that every guest is welcomed with effortless ease. Savor modern Italian flavors at Angeline's and sip craft cocktails from our rooftop bar, Merchant & Trade, perched high above Romare Bearden Park with sweeping skyline views. Your Uptown Charlotte experience starts here.

Heart of Uptown

Our downtown is called Uptown, matching the trajectory of this humble metropolis. Situated in Uptown’s 3rd Ward, Kimpton Tryon Park Hotel is steps from Charlotte’s major entertainment venues including the BB&T Ballpark and Bank of America Stadium and Spectrum Center, great for both work and play.
